Overview

This artifact is a reflective River of Life exercise that maps significant life experiences, transitions, relationships, values, and challenges across time. I included it because it highlights the role of reflection and self-awareness in my personal and professional development.

This artifact demonstrates my commitment to reflection as a foundation for effective practice.
