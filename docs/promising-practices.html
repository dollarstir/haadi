International Student Career Readiness

Group presentation proposing a career readiness program for international students at Indiana University (Artifact 10).

The project identifies a gap in career resources tailored to international students, defines a target audience, outlines program offerings, proposes campus partnerships, and includes evaluation methods and cost considerations—moving from idea to a structured student affairs initiative grounded in access, support, and sustainability.

My role was co-developing the presentation and contributing to program design to better equip international students for U.S. job search and interview processes. The artifact connects to leadership, advising and supporting, social justice and inclusion, and assessment and organizational thinking through proposed surveys, resource tracking, partnerships, and budget planning.
